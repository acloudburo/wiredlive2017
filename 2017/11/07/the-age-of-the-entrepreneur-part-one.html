PANEL: HOW DO WE BEST SOLVE THE BIGGEST CHALLENGES FACING OUR PLANET?



Niklas Zennström
CEO and founding partner, Atomico
Johan Rockström
director, Stockholm Resilience Centre

SUMMARY

Entrepreneurs and innovators are changing the world - there has never been a more exciting time to support and empower such change makers. We have cashed all our checks with mother nature, as seen with all the natural distastes happening all around the world. There is a pressing need for tackling environmental issues. However, for innovation and technology to have significant impacts on the issues, it needs the support of the greater ecosystem.
No single party alone can tackle the situation alone, it takes the formation of strong alliances working towards a common sustainable, ecological future.
