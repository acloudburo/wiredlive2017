SPEAKER BIO

In her award-winning debut film My Beautiful Broken Brain, film-maker Lotje Sodderland explores resilience and the mind following her recovery from an intracerebral haemorrhage.

SUMMARY

After surviving a brain hemorrhage, film-maker Lotje decided to record hours of video footage in order to express and share her experience, regain the ability to communicate and more so, turn a negative life event into a meaning life project.
